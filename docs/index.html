How It Works

1. Sign Up

Create an account with MailMouse and choose the subscription plan that best fits your needs. Our simple registration process gets you started in no time.

2. Create Your List

Set up your mailing list by defining a unique email address and adding your members' email addresses. Our intuitive interface makes list management a breeze.

3. Start Communicating

Send emails to your newly created list address, and MailMouse will automatically distribute them to all members. It's that simple!
